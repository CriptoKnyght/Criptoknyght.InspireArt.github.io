Jasper's AI art FAQs

Have any other questions? Visit our Jasper Art knowledge base or get in touch with our team at hey@jasper.ai
How much does Jasper Art cost?

Jasper Art is free to try for 5 days. During your free trial, you can generate up to 200 images at 2k high-resolution with no watermark. At the end of 5 days, Jasper Art costs $20/mo per user for unlimited art & image creation. There are no contracts and you can cancel anytime!

Start your free trial of Jasper Art here.

Does Art use credits? Is there a limit to how many images can create per month?

Jasper Art does not use credits. You can create unlimited images! :-)

Who owns the images Jasper Art creates?

During the Jasper Art beta, all images are creative commons. You are free to use the images however you want, but please know that your images could also be used by others. We are planning to revisit this, and hope to include optionality for you to own the images you create with Art in the future.

What commercial copyrights do I have on images generated by AI?

You can use the images you create using Jasper Art for your personal use and for any commercial projects that include those images. We don’t have any limitations on the types of commercial projects where you could use the images. You can reprint and sell the images as your imagination permits – in digital forms, on the internet, on your website or other sites, on t-shirts, on tote bags, on coffee mugs, you name it! – as long as you follow our terms and some basic rules.

What about biases in the images that are generated?  

If used incorrectly, Artificial intelligence can create images that reinforce stereotypes, under represent groups and are otherwise biased. We are always striving to improve the model to help mitigate societal biases, but it needs human intervention, judgment and compassion in the inputs and review of images generated. We ask you as a user to do your part to minimize bias.

What's the refund policy for Jasper Art?

We are not issuing refunds for Jasper Art. You can cancel at any time and use Jasper Art for the rest of the month you have paid for.

What's the cancelation policy for Jasper Art?

We're sad to see you go but thanks for giving Jasper Art a shot! 🚀

You can cancel your Jasper Art subscription at any time inside of the app. Canceling Jasper Art will not cancel your Jasper Write subscription.

To cancel, go to Billing in your settings and scroll to the Jasper Art subscription section. You will see a "cancel plan" button.

Cancellations will be automatically set for the end of your billing cycle. This ensures that you can still use Jasper Art for the month you have already paid for.

Can I publish AI art images on our site/blogs?

Yes! The images you create with Art can be used on blogs, websites, for creative, etc.

Please know that you are responsible for the content you post including its legality, reliability, and appropriateness. While Art encourages creativity, please respect the copyright of others when generating images. We discourage copyright infringement and ask that you are mindful of this when using Art.

Is there anything I’m not allowed to ask Jasper to create with Art?

Our AI does have a content filter. The filter's aim is to detect any generated text that could be sensitive or unsafe. Read more about our content filter here.

What rights do I have to use the images that Jasper AI creates with my input?

By using Jasper AI, and as long as you follow the rules and these terms, you basically have unlimited permissions to use the images commercially. For more legalese, subject to these commercial terms and our Terms of Service, you have a perpetual, worldwide, non-exclusive, no-charge, royalty-free, irrevocable copyright license to reproduce, prepare, publicly display, publicly perform, distribute, and create derivative works based on those images.

Who else can use my images?  

Since Jasper Art is publicly available for the Internet community, by default your images are publicly viewable and remixable. You grant Jasper Art and its customers the same permissions to use any images that Jasper Art generated with your input as Jasper Art grants you to use those images. In other words, you grant us and our other users a perpetual, worldwide, non-exclusive, no-charge, royalty-free, irrevocable copyright license to reproduce, prepare, publicly display, publicly perform, distribute, and create derivative works based on those images.

As of now, the US Copyright office has refused to grant copyright protections to AI generated images.

So I don’t have exclusive rights to my images?

Our community is about sharing, and the way our platform works is that any content generated by Jasper Art is free for anyone to use, as long as you follow these terms. And if one of our other users happened to use the exact same inputs and got identical or nearly-identical results, they can also use their resulting image for commercial purposes just like you can.
